About Us

Andrea Vimercati, master in molecolar biology, has been working for twenty years in the Pharmaceuticals and Diagnostic industry, collaborating with players such as Becton Dickinson, Novartis, Grifols, Pfizer, Menarini.
Andrea started his career as a pharmaceutical sales representative, to become later product manager. In the diagnostic sector, he started as a salesman, increasing his responsibilities year by year, achieving the role of sales manager for one of the biggest player in the industry. In January 2020, just before his 45 y.o. birthday, he founded VIMEX s.r.l., through which he partners with the top companies in the Diagnostic industry.
